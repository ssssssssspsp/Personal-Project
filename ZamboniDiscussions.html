Questions and Answers

What is Sean's favorite color?

Judging from the color of the walls in his bedroom, Sean's favorite color is blue.

What is Sean's favorite TV show?

While Sean doesn't have a solidified favorite TV show, he is currently watching Community and Suits.

What car does Sean drive?

Sean used to drive a 2013 Jeep Grand Cherokee when he first got his license. It had over 150,000 miles and seemed to break down everyday he tried to drive it. Functionally, it was one of the worst cars he has ever seen, but it had a lot of character and Sean was not happy to let it go when it broke down for the final time. Now, acquired by trading in the Jeep, he drives a Cadillac XTS with 100,000 miles which seems to have no character or life to it at all.

What is Sean's favorite season of the year?

Sean's favorite season of the year is summer becuase he likes the nice weather and the availability to be outside all the time.

What is Sean's favorite place to eat.

Like a moth to a flame, Sean goes to either Chick Fil A or Chipotle after most of his shifts. Ultimately Chipotle is his favorite.
